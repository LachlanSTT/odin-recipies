Pumpkin Soup

Description

This Pumpkin Soup recipe is of easy difficulty. It has a prep time of 10 minutes. A total time of 30 minutes. It has a serving size of 4 portions.

Ingredients:

  • 1 brown onion (approx. 180 g), cut into halves
  • 20 g butter
  • 500 g pumpkin, peeled, cut into pieces
  • 1 carrot, cut into pieces
  • 500 g water, adjust to taste
  • 1 tbsp Vegetable stock paste (see Tips)
  • 30 g pouring (whipping) cream (optional)
  • 2 pinches salt, to taste
  • 2 pinches ground black pepper, to taste
  • fresh basil, leaves only, for garnishing
